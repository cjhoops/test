Why Us

Why Us

WordPress, Wix, and other drag-and-drop page builders slow down your site due to extraneous code, and push your site to the bottom of search engine results due to lack of keywords. This is why each of our websites are hand-coded to optimize load times for customers, and to make your website show up higher in search engines.

  • Improved User Experience

    Nearly 70% of consumers admit that webpage speed impacts their willingness to buy from a business. (Unbounce, 2019)

  • Higher Conversion Rates

    Conversion rates (the rate of site visitors who end up making a purchase) are 3x higher for ecommerce sites that load in 1 second, compared to sites that load in 5 seconds (Portent, 2020)

  • Trust and Credibility

    Websites that rank higher in search results are often perceived as more trustworthy and credible by users. Being among the top search results implies to users that your content is relevant and reliable.

  • Increased Visibility

    Having a high search engine ranking results in more clicks to your business's website. This means more potential customers are likely to discover your website, increasing revenue.

  • Positive Brand Perception

    A fast website reflects positively on your business. Visitors associate a fast-loading site with professionalism, reliability, and efficiency, enhancing your brand's reputation both online and in-person.

  • Competitive Advantage

    Outranking competitors in search results will always give you an advantage over local competitors. When searching for a product or service in their area, potential customers will be exposed to your business first.
